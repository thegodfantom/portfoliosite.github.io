Anime

Hunter x Hunter

A brilliant subversion of classic shonen tropes. It follows Gon Freecss on his journey to find his father, leading him into the dangerous world of Hunters. Renowned for its incredibly detailed power system (Nen) and phenomenal character writing, particularly in the dark Chimera Ant arc.

Berserk

The absolute pinnacle of dark fantasy. Following Guts, the Black Swordsman, in a relentless and brutal struggle against causality, demons, and his former commanding officer. A gritty, philosophical masterpiece about human willpower.

Bleach

With unmatched drip and one of the best soundtracks in anime history, this follows Ichigo Kurosaki as he is thrust into the role of a Substitute Soul Reaper. Massive swords, Hollows, and incredible battles in the Soul Society make this a classic.

Vinland Saga

A profound historical epic set during the Viking Age. It masterfully transitions from a story of pure, bloodthirsty revenge to a deeply emotional journey about redemption, pacifism, and what it truly means to be a warrior.

Death Note

A high-stakes, supernatural cat-and-mouse thriller. Light Yagami discovers a notebook that kills anyone whose name is written in it, leading to a brilliant psychological war of wits against the eccentric detective known only as 'L'.

Naruto

The defining ninja epic. From a lonely outcast pulling pranks to a hero fighting to protect his village, Naruto's journey is filled with iconic jutsu, complex villains, and unforgettable emotional beats.

Hajime no Ippo

Pure, unadulterated hype. Watching Ippo Makunouchi transform from a bullied high schooler into a terrifying force in the professional boxing ring is incredibly inspiring. The sound design during the fights hits as hard as a Dempsey Roll.

The Eminence in Shadow

A hilarious and stylish isekai that completely owns its edgy premise. Cid Kagenou doesn't want to be the hero or the villain; he just wants to be the ultimate mastermind operating from the shadows—and he's playing the role perfectly.
